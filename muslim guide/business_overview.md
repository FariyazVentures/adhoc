An app designed for the Islamic religion can include features that cater to the spiritual, educational, and daily needs of Muslims. Here are some common features such an app might need:
1. Prayer Times
Accurate prayer times based on the user's location.
Notifications or reminders for each prayer (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha).
Support for different calculation methods (e.g., Umm Al-Qura, ISNA).

2. Qibla Direction
A compass to show the direction of the Kaaba in Mecca.
Integration with GPS for accurate Qibla direction.

3. Quran
Full Quran text with translations in multiple languages.
Audio recitations by different Qaris (reciters).
Tafsir (exegesis) for deeper understanding.

4. Dua and Supplications
A collection of daily duas (supplications) for various occasions.
Categorized duas (e.g., morning, evening, travel, etc.).

5. Islamic Calendar
Hijri calendar with important Islamic dates (e.g., Ramadan, Eid, Hajj).
Event reminders for significant days.

6. Zakat Calculator
A tool to calculate Zakat based on the user's assets and liabilities.
Guidelines for Zakat calculation.

7. Halal Food Finder
A feature to locate halal restaurants and stores nearby.
Barcode scanner to check if a product is halal.

8. Hajj and Umrah Guide
Step-by-step guide for performing Hajj and Umrah.
Checklist for preparation and rituals.

9. Islamic Knowledge
Articles, videos, and lectures on Islamic teachings.
Stories of Prophets and Sahabah (companions of the Prophet).

10. Community Features
Nearby mosque locator with prayer times and events.
Community forums or groups for discussions.

11. Parental and Family Features
Islamic content for children (e.g., stories, games, and quizzes).
Family prayer tracking or shared goals.

12. Customization
Ability to choose themes (e.g., light/dark mode).
Customizable notification sounds (e.g., Adhan).

13. Privacy and Security
Ensure user data is secure and private.
Avoid collecting unnecessary personal information.

14. Multilingual Support
Support for multiple languages to cater to a global audience.

15. Offline Mode
Allow access to Quran, duas, and other essential features without an internet connection.

16. Guide: What is Islam and aspects. Step by Step guide to proceed.
